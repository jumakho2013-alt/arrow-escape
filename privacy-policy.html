Stray Arrows - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

Stray Arrows is a puzzle game that respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Stray Arrows does not collect any personal information. All game progress (levels, coins, achievements, statistics) is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Stray Arrows does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Internet Connection

Stray Arrows works fully offline. An internet connection is only required for the initial download. No data is sent or received during gameplay.

Children's Privacy

Stray Arrows does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The game is suitable for all ages.

Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted here.
